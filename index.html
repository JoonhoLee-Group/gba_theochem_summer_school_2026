Overview

Training the next generation of theoretical chemists

The Greater Boston Area Theoretical Chemistry Summer School brings together leading instructors from the Boston area for five days of lectures, discussions, and hands-on learning. The school is designed to give participants a broad view of contemporary theoretical chemistry while building practical intuition across complementary fields.

Participants will engage with core concepts and current research directions in four focus areas: electronic structure and quantum information, statistical mechanics, quantum dynamics, and biosimulations with machine learning.

Registration

Applications will be reviewed by the organizing committee.

Applications are collected through Google Forms. The form asks for your name, university, advisor information, CV, and advisor endorsement.

The registration fee is $50. Payment is required only for applicants who are accepted to the summer school. A payment link will be sent to accepted participants in July 2026.

Please note that travel, lodging, and meal support will not be provided. Participants are responsible for arranging and covering their own travel, accommodations, and meals.
